About Ghislain

Ghislain is a dedicated logistics manager with a wealth of experience in various organizations. He is passionate about making a difference in humanitarian efforts.

Ghislain Kisamba Shabani, armed with his education in International Solidarity Logistics from Institut Bioforce-France and certifications in Management and Leadership, has dedicated a significant part of his life to an exceptional professional career in the field of humanitarian logistics.

With over 6 years of experience as a Logistics Manager, notably within the Norwegian Refugee Council, he has demonstrated his ability to manage large teams and ensure the efficient coordination of complex logistics operations.

Furthermore, his strong educational background and diverse professional journey as a Logistics Coordinator and Logistics Officer reflect his unwavering commitment to improving humanitarian operations and his enlightened leadership in this crucial field.
