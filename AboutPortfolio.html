About Ghislain Kisamba

Ghislain is a logistics professional with a degree in International Solidarity Logistics from Institut Bioforce in France. Furthermore, he is certified in Management and Leadership, as well as in Humanitarian Project Design and Management, all obtained in France.
His impressive professional career spans more than 8 years as a Logistics Manager, where he offered his expertise to the Norwegian Refugee Council Mission in Niger, Cameroon, the DRC, and other countries.

As a logistician, Ghislain demonstrates exceptional management of complexity, which is essential for coordinating logistics operations in sometimes challenging environments. He displays great flexibility in adapting quickly to unforeseen situations and emergencies, a crucial skill in the humanitarian field.

His keen organizational sense allows him to efficiently manage stocks, supplies, and a team of human resources while maintaining the necessary structure. Ghislain is also an effective communicator, capable of coordinating with other teams, partners, and agencies to ensure the success of operations.
His analytical skills enable him to make informed decisions by examining data, costs, timelines, and risks. Additionally, he effectively manages stress related to emergency situations, which is essential for maintaining operational stability.

With a strong technical knowledge of logistics tools, supply chain management systems, and regulations, Ghislain ensures that operations run smoothly.
He pays meticulous attention to detail to avoid costly errors, demonstrating his commitment to excellence in his field.
Finally, Ghislain maintains a strict adherence to ethics, crucial for managing precious resources dedicated to humanitarian missions.
His solid education and impressive career as a Logistics Coordinator reflect his exceptional contribution to the success of humanitarian operations across various African countries.
