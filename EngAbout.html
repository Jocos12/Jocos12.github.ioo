About Us

Maison ECAL and Divers specializes in various logistics functions, providing innovative solutions to clients. We take pride in our expertise in order management and transportation.

Logistics is at the core of our business, ensuring smooth and efficient logistic support in various tasks. At Maison ECAL, we assist you in inventory management, supply chain coordination, and various logistics tasks, all with a commitment to ensuring customer satisfaction.

Mission of Maison ECAL:

"Our mission at Maison ECAL is to leverage our logistics expertise to offer innovative solutions in order management, transportation, inventory management, and supply chain coordination.

We are committed to providing smooth and efficient logistic support in various tasks to meet our clients' needs.

Our ultimate goal is to ensure customer satisfaction by offering high-quality logistics services."

Vision of Maison ECAL:

"At Maison ECAL, we aspire to become the preferred partner for our clients in logistics. Our vision is to be recognized as industry leaders, offering advanced logistics services that anticipate market needs.

We aim to continue innovating in our logistics processes, improving inventory management, optimizing supply chains, and reducing the environmental impact of our operations.

We firmly believe that every interaction with our clients should be marked by efficiency, reliability, and total satisfaction, strengthening our reputation for logistical excellence."
